Product discovery

All products are built on innovative ideas. But all great products are built on validated ideas. So, when you approach us with an idea on a napkin, we help you discover and define if the product has a strong market demand. Through product discovery workshops, we uncover how the product can create significant customer value.

But we don't pull it out thin air. We sit with you and understand your business problems, product's vision, and time constraints. Once we have a clear understanding, we help you define your design strategy, development roadmap, and a broad customer experience.

How we do it?

Product discovery workshop

We start with a hair-on-fire problem and define a targeted group of people who might be facing it. This involves listing down the attributes of various target users and what kind of problems they face. We move quickly in weekly sprints by defining our value proposition and outlining a plan for how our product will meet users’ needs better than what already exists in the market.

  • Stakeholders interviews
  • Product canvas map
  • Persona analysis
  • Empathy maps
  • UX strategy map
  • Use case scenario mapping
  • Primary noun table
  • High level functionality matrix

Experience design

A great experience remains etched in the memory of a user, just as the memory of their first visit to Disneyland. Let that thought sink in! It’s a critical step in building trust and lasting relationships.

We help you build that connection by designing experiences that are not just outwardly beautiful, but also solve the most vexing problems of the users. But users never tell what hurts them most? Well, that’s kinda true. How we discover those problems is a story for another day!
