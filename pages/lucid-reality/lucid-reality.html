An Overview

Are you dreaming? Or, are you Lucid Dreaming?

Lucid dreaming is a dream where you’re aware of everything you see and experience. In fact, a person can also control what happens in the dream. This typically happens during REM sleep, one of the dream-stages.

  • The onboarding flow is simple and intuitive. Users can login using their Google account and navigate through the app easily.
  • Each task is divided into smodular steps that helps users move faster, without getting overwhelmed by information overload.
  • The use of sans-serif font is by choice to simprove readability and clarity. Since the users will be using the app at all times- during the daytime as well as night, we didn't want them to lose their attention to curvy font styles.
  • The buttons and cards are ergonomically placed so that it's easier for the user to tap and swipe without using the other hand.
  • We’ve designed the app keeping in mind the environment in which end users will use the application. Therefore, we’ve designed multiple ways (voice, text and sketch) to record and maintain a dream journal.

The Solution

Designed for every lucid dreamer persona

The targeted audience of our app is either pro-lucid dreamers or beginners who are new to the realm of lucid dreaming. So, the user experience is designed to make it easy for every user persona.

  • The onboarding flow is simple and intuitive. Users can login using their Google account and navigate through the app easily.
  • Each task is divided into smodular steps that helps users move faster, without getting overwhelmed by information overload.
  • The use of sans-serif font is by choice to simprove readability and clarity. Since the users will be using the app at all times- during the daytime as well as night, we didn't want them to lose their attention to curvy font styles.

development

Coding the experience

Since we were developing the MVP of this app, we followed the iterative and incremental development process to roll out this project. We worked in 2 weeks design and development sprints.

In our design sprints, as we progressed from paper wireframes to prototypes, we also started working on the visual design. Meanwhile, we took feedback from all stakeholders and kept optimizing the controls and interactions of the app.

In development sprints, our mobile team worked on the iOS platform to build this app. The backend is built in Firebase. We built the whole app from ground-up in just 3 weeks.
